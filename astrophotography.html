Passionate about exploring the cosmos through astronomy and capturing celestial objects through astrophotography. Organized astronomy and astrophotography events through outreach programs to inspire students in STEM education.

Orion Nebula (M42/M43) & Horsehead Nebula

A stunning view of the Orion Nebula, one of the brightest star-forming regions, alongside the iconic Horsehead Nebula silhouetted against the glowing emission nebula IC 434. This region showcases active stellar formation in vibrant purples, reds, and oranges.

Trifid Nebula (M20)

The Trifid Nebula displays a beautiful mix of emission and reflection nebulosity. Dark dust lanes divide the glowing red hydrogen gas into three distinct lobes, while blue starlight reflects off dust clouds, creating a striking cosmic landscape.

Heart Nebula (IC 1805)

The iconic Heart Nebula in Cassiopeia, glowing with vibrant reds, oranges, and blues. This vast emission nebula is illuminated by a cluster of young, hot stars at its core, showcasing active stellar formation and the intricate beauty of cosmic clouds.

Sadr Region (IC 1318)

A wide-field view of the Gamma Cygni Nebula complex in Cygnus, featuring intricate butterfly-like structures. The nebula glows with electric blue ionized oxygen at its core, transitioning to golden-orange and reddish-brown hydrogen emissions, all set against a dense star field.

Rosette Nebula (NGC 2237)

A vibrant deep-sky object featuring a bright teal and blue core surrounded by warm oranges and browns. The nebula's complex structure includes delicate filaments and dark dust lanes, all set within a dense field of stars, highlighting the dynamic processes of star formation.

Pacman Nebula (NGC 281)

A captivating emission nebula in Cassiopeia resembling the classic video game character. This star-forming region glows with brilliant reds and oranges from ionized hydrogen, with dark dust lanes creating the distinctive "mouth" shape against the luminous gas clouds.
